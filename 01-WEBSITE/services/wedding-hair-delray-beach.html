Wedding Hair FAQs

How far in advance should I book my wedding hair?

We recommend booking 3-6 months before your wedding date, especially for peak season (October-May in Florida). Schedule your trial 2-4 weeks before the wedding.

Do you offer trial sessions for brides?

Yes! We strongly recommend a trial session where we can perfect your look, try different styles, and ensure everything is exactly how you envision. This is included in our bridal package.

Can you come to my venue on the wedding day?

Absolutely! We offer on-location services throughout Delray Beach, Boca Raton, and Palm Beach County. We bring all equipment and create a relaxed, stress-free experience.

How do you handle Florida's humidity?

With 20+ years in South Florida, we're experts at creating styles that last through humidity, beach ceremonies, and outdoor receptions. We use professional-grade products and techniques specifically designed for our climate.

What should I bring to my trial session?

Bring photos of styles you like, your veil or headpiece if you have one, and wear a top that's similar to your dress neckline. We'll work together to create your perfect wedding day look.
