Hair Extensions FAQs

Will extensions damage my natural hair?

When properly installed and maintained by a certified professional like Chris, extensions should not damage your hair. The key is correct placement, appropriate weight, and regular maintenance appointments.

How long do extensions last?

Depends on the method: Tape-ins last 6-8 weeks between moves, beaded weft 8-12 weeks, fusion 3-4 months. With proper care, the hair itself can be reused for 6-12 months.

Can I swim with extensions?

Yes! We recommend wetting your hair before entering pools or ocean, and washing with clarifying shampoo after. Chris will teach you proper care for Florida's beach lifestyle.

Which method is best for me?

It depends on your hair type, lifestyle, and goals. During your consultation, Chris will examine your hair and recommend the best method for your unique situation.

How do I care for my extensions?

Use sulfate-free products, brush gently from ends to roots, sleep with hair braided or in a silk bonnet, and keep up with maintenance appointments. Chris provides a full care guide at installation.
