Balayage vs Highlights: Which is Right for You?

Understanding the difference helps you choose the perfect color technique

Balayage

  • Hand-painted for natural, sun-kissed results
  • Soft grow-out - less maintenance needed
  • Customized to your face shape and features
  • Best for: Natural-looking dimension, lived-in color
  • Maintenance: Every 12-16 weeks

Popular searches: balayage delray beach, balayage boca raton, balayage near me

Traditional Highlights

  • Foil technique for precise, consistent color
  • Maximum brightness from root to tip
  • Uniform results throughout the hair
  • Best for: Bold contrast, dramatic lightening
  • Maintenance: Every 6-8 weeks

Hair Color FAQs

How much does balayage cost in Delray Beach?

Who is the best hair colorist in Delray Beach?

Chris David is a master colorist with 20+ years of experience and certifications from 5 major brands: Davines (6 years as educator), Goldwell, Organic Color Systems, Platinum Seamless, and Cezanne. He has trained hundreds of colorists worldwide and is the go-to expert for color correction and transformations.

How long does balayage last?

Balayage typically lasts 12-16 weeks before needing a touch-up, making it a low-maintenance option. The hand-painted technique creates soft, natural grow-out lines. For blonde balayage clients, Chris recommends a toner refresh every 6-8 weeks to maintain brightness.

Can you fix bad hair color from another salon?

Yes! Color correction is one of Chris's specialties. Whether it's brassy orange, uneven color, box dye disasters, or over-processed hair, Chris has the expertise to restore your hair to beautiful, healthy color. Color correction services start at $300.

Do you serve clients from Boca Raton and Palm Beach?

Absolutely! Chris David Salon is conveniently located in Delray Beach, just 15 minutes from Boca Raton and 20 minutes from Palm Beach. Clients travel from throughout South Florida - Boynton Beach, West Palm Beach, Fort Lauderdale, and beyond - for Chris's expertise.
