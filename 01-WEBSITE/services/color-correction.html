Color Correction FAQ

How long does color correction take?

Depending on the severity, corrections can take 3-8 hours. Complex cases may require multiple sessions.

Will color correction damage my hair?

We use gentle, professional products including Olaplex to protect your hair throughout the process.

Can you fix box dye?

Yes! Box dye correction is one of our specialties, though it may require multiple sessions.

How much does it cost?

Corrections start at $185 and can go up to $685+ for extensive work. We provide exact quotes during consultation.
