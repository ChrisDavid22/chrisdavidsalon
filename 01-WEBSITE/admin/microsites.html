You Can't See Your Leads Without GA4

Your microsites are live and sending visitors to your main site, but without Google Analytics connected, we can't tell you how many.

To connect GA4 (takes 15 minutes):

  1. 1
    Get your GA4 Property ID
    Go to analytics.google.com → Admin → Property Settings → Copy the Property ID (looks like: 123456789)
  2. 2
    Create a Service Account
    Go to Google Cloud Console → Create service account → Download JSON key
  3. 3
    Add to Vercel
    In Vercel dashboard, add environment variables:
    GA4_PROPERTY_ID = your property ID
    GOOGLE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON = paste entire JSON key

Need help? Ask your developer or email the GA4 admin for your account.

How to fix it (takes 5 minutes):

  1. 1 Go to Google Search Console
  2. 2 Click "Add Property" and enter: bestdelraysalon.com
  3. 3 Verify ownership (use the DNS method if prompted)

How Do Microsites Help Your Business?

1. More Keywords = More Visibility

Each microsite targets different keywords that your main site might not rank for. "best salon delray" → bestsalondelray.com → sends visitors to chrisdavidsalon.com

2. Backlinks = Higher Authority

Each microsite links to your main site. Google sees this as a vote of confidence, helping chrisdavidsalon.com rank higher overall.

3. Direct Leads

Someone searching "best salon palm beach" finds bestsalonpalmbeach.com, clicks through to your site, and books an appointment. That's a lead.

4. Long-Term Investment

As these sites age and build authority, they become more valuable. A 2-year-old microsite ranks better than a new one.
