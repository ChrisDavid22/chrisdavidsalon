üìà Microsite Success Formula

What Makes a Profitable Microsite

  • Keyword Volume: 200+ searches/month minimum
    Below this, ROI is difficult
  • Competition: Low to medium difficulty
    Can rank #1 within 60 days
  • Commercial Intent: Service-specific keywords
    "balayage" beats "hair ideas"
  • Content Depth: 5+ pages of unique content
    Homepage, service, gallery, about, contact

Optimization Checklist

  • Technical Setup: Fast hosting, SSL, mobile-ready
    Use same hosting as main site
  • Local SEO: NAP consistency, schema markup
    Same address/phone as main
  • Link Strategy: 3-5 links to main site
    Natural, contextual links
  • Tracking: Unique phone number per site
    Track actual conversions
