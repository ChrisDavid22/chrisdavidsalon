Color Correction FAQ

How long does color correction take?

Most color corrections take 3-6 hours depending on the severity. Complex corrections may require multiple sessions spaced 2-4 weeks apart to maintain hair health.

Will color correction damage my hair?

When done by a Master Colorist using professional products, damage is minimized. We always prioritize hair health and include deep conditioning treatments with every correction.

Can you fix box dye?

Yes! Box dye corrections are one of our specialties. The metallic salts in box dye require special techniques, which we're expertly trained to handle.

How soon can I get an appointment?

We reserve slots for color correction emergencies. Most clients can be seen within 3-5 days. Call (561) 299-0950 for fastest scheduling.

What if I'm not happy with the correction?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy, we'll adjust the color free of charge within 7 days of your service.
