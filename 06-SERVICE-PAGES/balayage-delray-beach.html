What Makes Our Balayage Special?

Balayage is a French hair coloring technique that creates natural, graduated highlights through hand-painting. Unlike traditional foil highlights, balayage offers a more natural, sun-kissed look that grows out beautifully without harsh lines.

Balayage FAQ

How long does balayage last?

Balayage typically lasts 3-4 months, growing out naturally without harsh lines. Touch-ups can extend the look even longer.

How much does balayage cost at Chris David Salon?

Balayage services start at $200+, with final pricing based on hair length, thickness, and desired result. We offer free consultations.

Is balayage damaging to hair?

When done correctly with quality products like Davines, balayage is less damaging than traditional highlights. We include treatments to maintain hair health.

How long does a balayage appointment take?

Plan for 2-4 hours depending on your hair length and the complexity of your desired look. We never rush perfection.
